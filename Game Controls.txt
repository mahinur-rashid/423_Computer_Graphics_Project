
## Player Controls

### Player 1:

- **W**: Move up
- **S**: Move down
- **A**: Move left
- **D**: Move right
- **SPACE**: Shoot

### Player 2:

- **I**: Move up
- **K**: Move down
- **J**: Move left
- **L**: Move right
- **P**: Shoot


## Top buttons

- Left-most button icon*: Restart
- right-most button icon*: Exit
- middle button icon*: Play/Pause

## keyboard functionalities

- ESC key*: Play/Pause
